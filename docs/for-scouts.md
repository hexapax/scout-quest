# Scout Quest: Quick Start for Scouts

## What is Scout Quest?

Scout Quest is an AI assistant built for our troop. It knows your real advancement data from Scoutbook — your rank, merit badges, completed requirements, and upcoming events. You can ask it questions and get answers based on your actual records.

Think of it as a coach that knows the BSA handbook inside and out AND knows where you are in your scouting journey.

## How to Use It

1. Go to **scout-quest.hexapax.com**
2. Log in with your Google account (the one linked to your Scoutbook profile)
3. Start chatting

No special commands. Just type what you want to know.

## Things You Can Ask

**Your progress:**
- "What rank am I working on?"
- "What do I still need for First Class?"
- "How many merit badges have I earned?"
- "What Eagle-required badges do I still need?"

**BSA rules and requirements:**
- "What are the Second Class requirements?"
- "Do my partial completions expire?" (Nope.)
- "What's the buddy system?"
- "How does a Board of Review work?"

**Working with other scouts:**
- "Who else is working on the same rank as me?"
- "Who can help me with my requirements?"
- "What could I teach younger scouts?"
- "What can me and Henry work on together?"

**Events:**
- "What's the next troop event?"
- "When is the campout?"

## Finding Buddies

One of the best features: Scout Quest can match you with other scouts to work together.

- **Need help?** Ask "Who can help me with first aid?" and it'll find scouts who already passed those requirements and could practice with you.
- **Want to help?** Ask "What could I teach?" and it'll show you younger scouts working on requirements you've already completed. Teaching is one of the best ways to lock in what you've learned — and it counts toward leadership.
- **Working with a friend?** Ask "What can me and [friend's name] work on together?" and it'll find requirements you're both working on or could help each other with.

## What It Won't Do

- **It won't do your work.** It'll ask you questions and help you think through problems, but YOU do the work. That's the whole point of scouting.
- **It won't sign off on requirements.** Only your Scoutmaster or a merit badge counselor can do that. Scout Quest helps you prepare — you still need to demonstrate the skill to a leader.
- **It won't write your Eagle project for you.** It can help you plan and organize, but the project is yours.

## Tips

- **Be specific.** "What do I need for First Class?" works better than "What should I do?"
- **Ask follow-up questions.** If it gives you a list of requirements, ask about the ones you don't understand.
- **Use it before meetings.** Check what you're working on so you can make the most of troop time.
- **Ask about the EDGE method.** If you're teaching someone else (which you'll need to do for Life rank), the AI can help you plan your lesson.

## Questions?

Ask Scout Quest: **"What can you help me with?"** — or talk to your Scoutmaster.
