As of 2026, **Eagle Scout candidates must earn 21 merit badges total, with 13 required from a specific list and 8 electives**[1][3].

## Current Eagle-Required Merit Badges (2026)

The **13 required merit badges** are[3]:

1. Camping
2. Citizenship in the Community
3. Citizenship in the Nation
4. Citizenship in the World
5. Communication
6. Cooking
7. **Cycling** OR **Hiking** OR **Swimming** (choose one)
8. **Emergency Preparedness** OR **Lifesaving** (choose one)
9. **Environmental Science** OR **Sustainability** (choose one)
10. Family Life
11. First Aid
12. Personal Fitness
13. Personal Management

The remaining 8 merit badges can be any badges the Scout chooses[1][3].

## Recent Changes: Citizenship in Society Removal

**Citizenship in Society was discontinued effective February 27, 2026**[1]. This represents a significant shift:

- **Before January 1, 2026:** Eagle required 14 specific merit badges (including Citizenship in Society), with 7 electives
- **As of January 1, 2026 onward:** Eagle requires 13 specific merit badges (the traditional "Three Citizenships" framework), with 8 electives[1]

### Transition Rules for Scouts

**For Scouts who started Citizenship in Society before February 27, 2026:**[1]

- They have until **December 31, 2026** to complete the badge
- If completed by that deadline, it can count toward Eagle rank

**For Scouts starting after February 27, 2026:**[1]

- They may no longer begin this merit badge

No new Eagle-required badges were added or removed beyond the reinstatement of the traditional Three Citizenships structure. The emphasis on **Sustainability** (as an alternative to Environmental Science) reflects the modernization updates effective January 1, 2026[1].
