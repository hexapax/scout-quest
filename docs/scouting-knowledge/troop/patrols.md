## Patrol Method

- The troop has been working to implement the Scout-led patrol method progressively since 2024
- Jeremy Bramwell reiterated at the August 2024 meeting that moving to Scout-led format "is a journey"
- Patrols elect their own Patrol Leaders
- SPL is elected by all Scouts
- SPL appoints all other positions of responsibility
- Leadership meeting agendas have repeatedly discussed patrol method improvements and how to organize new Scouts
- PLC (Patrol Leaders Council) meetings discussed but not consistently scheduled monthly as planned

## Expectations for Life Scouts

As established at the August 2024 leadership meeting: Life Scouts must continue to participate in the troop even after completing their 6 months of Position of Responsibility. This was confirmed by other troop leaders at Woodruff and represents a change from the troop's original practice (where even Eagle Scouts stayed on for the rest of the school year under David Owens' leadership).
