# Troop 2024 Overview

## Identity

- **Troop Number:** 2024
- **Charter Organization:** Pace Academy (private school in Atlanta, GA)
- **Location:** Buckhead area, Atlanta, Georgia
- **Council:** Atlanta Area Council
- **District:** Phoenix District
- **Founded:** The troop originated from Pace Academy's Cub Scout Pack 4

## Meeting Schedule

- **Frequency:** Every other Tuesday night (biweekly)
- **Time:** 6:30-7:30 PM (changed from 6:00-7:00 PM after the 2022-2023 year)
- **Location:** Pace Academy, typically in Knights Hall
  - Occasional meetings held at alternate locations (e.g., Baby Braithwaite store for Fall Fair service project prep)
- **Food:** Pizza is provided for Scouts at meetings
- **Meeting Schedule (2025-2026):** Meetings are listed on Scoutbook calendar; parents/Scouts RSVP via Scoutbook

### Board of Review Sessions (New in 2026)

- Offered after the 2nd troop meeting of each month (approximately 7:30-8:00 PM)
- Scouts must RSVP in advance; if no one signs up, the BOR session is cancelled
- Each BOR takes approximately 10-15 minutes per Scout

### Court of Honor

- Held approximately twice per year (fall and spring)
- Spring 2026 COH: April 14 at 6:30 PM in Knights Hall
- Class A uniforms required
- Deadline for rank/MB submissions: one week before COH

## Communication

- **Primary Platform:** Scoutbook (migrated from Teamsnap + Troopmaster in late 2025)
  - Calendar events, RSVPs, advancement tracking, messaging all consolidated into Scoutbook
  - URL: https://advancements.scouting.org/calendar
- **Previous Tools (discontinued):** Teamsnap (calendar/RSVP), Troopmaster (advancement tracking)
- **Newsletter:** "Scout Stuff" email newsletter sent periodically (monthly or bimonthly)
- **Troop Email:** PaceTroop2024@gmail.com (set up ~2023)

## Troop Size

- As of September 2025 roster: 65 Scouts ever registered, with approximately 20-25 active
- Mix of active Scouts across ranks from New/Scout through Eagle
- 16 Eagle Scouts as of the 2025 roster (many have aged out)

## Uniforms

- **Class A (Field Uniform):** Official BSA uniform, required for Court of Honor and certain events (merit badge clinics, ceremonies at summer camp)
- **Class B (Activity Uniform):** Troop 2024 t-shirt, worn at regular biweekly meetings
- **Neckerchief:** Navy blue handkerchief with silver lining along the edge
- **Scout Shop:** Atlanta Area Boy Scout Shop at 1800 Circle 75 Pkwy, Atlanta, GA 30339

## Key Characteristics

- School-affiliated troop: most Scouts attend Pace Academy
- Troop aims for one campout per month during the school year
- Strong emphasis on Scout-led model (transitioning progressively since 2024)
- Parents welcome to camp with the troop; two-deep leadership required for all campouts
- Service hours through Pace/ICGL are pre-approved for rank advancement
- Service hours cannot be double-counted for both Pace and Scouts
