# Troop 2024 Finances

## Dues Structure

### Troop Activity Dues (paid to Pace Academy)

- **2026 amount:** $250/year per Scout
- **Payment methods:** Check payable to Pace Academy (mailed to Treasurer) or Venmo
- **Current Treasurer:** Eric Buffenbarger (@Eric-Buffenbarger on Venmo; 865 Moores Mill Road NW, Atlanta, GA 30327-1526)
- **Note:** Troop dues are separate from BSA annual registration fees paid directly to Scouting America

### BSA Registration Fee

- **Annual fee:** $75 paid directly to BSA
- **One-time new member fee:** $25 (waived if transitioning from Cub Scouts)
- Scouts register individually with BSA and pay this separately

### What Troop Dues Cover

- Campsite fees
- Groceries and firewood for campouts
- First aid supplies
- Registration of adult chaperones for events like Woodruff
- Supplies for troop meetings
- Pizza served at troop meetings
- Software subscriptions (historically Troopmaster and Teamsnap; now Scoutbook which is free)

### Woodruff Summer Camp (Paid Separately)

- **2026 cost:** $525 per Scout
- Paid by participants, not from troop budget
- Includes one high adventure activity (horseback riding or whitewater rafting)
- Additional high adventure: $50/activity
- Adult participation in high adventure: $50/activity

## Financial Policies

### Reimbursement Process

- Submit reimbursement requests to the Treasurer with attached invoice/receipt
- Amount must match invoice/receipt
- Do not intermingle scouting expenses with personal expenses
- Items and costs must be legible (scan/email preferred over photos)

### Cancelled Campout Policy (established August 2024)

- Troop absorbs campsite cancellation fees
- Reimburse trip captain for food that is not shelf-stable
- Cancellation fee: ~$250 if cancelled more than 1 week out
- If cancelled within 1 week: site fee increases from $10 to 50% of cost

### Pizza Budget

- $66 per meeting (as of August 2024)
- Pizza is provided for Scouts at biweekly meetings
- Jeremy and Colleen submit reimbursement for pizza costs
