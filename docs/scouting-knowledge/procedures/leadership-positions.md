**Approved leadership positions for Star, Life, and Eagle rank requirements in Scouting America Scouts BSA (formerly BSA) are listed in the official Scouts BSA Handbook and detailed on Scouting.org. These include specific troop positions and certain non-troop roles like those in Venturing crews or packs, with minimum service of 4 months for Star/Life (active and fulfilling duties) and 6 months for Eagle (same criteria).** [5][8]

### Troop Positions (All Count for Star, Life, and Eagle Unless Noted)
The following positions are universally approved across official and troop resources, appointed or elected by troop leadership:
- **Senior Patrol Leader** (elected)[1][2][3]
- **Assistant Senior Patrol Leader** (appointed by SPL)[1][3][4]
- **Patrol Leader** (elected; APL does **not** count)[1][2][3]
- **Troop Guide** (appointed by Scoutmaster)[1][2][3]
- **Order of the Arrow Troop Representative** (appointed by SPL)[1][2][3]
- **Scribe** (appointed by SPL)[1][2][3]
- **Librarian** (appointed by SPL)[1][3]
- **Historian** (appointed by SPL)[1][3]
- **Quartermaster** (appointed by SPL; troop-level, not patrol)[1][2][3]
- **Chaplain Aide** (appointed by SPL)[1][3]
- **Instructor** (appointed by Scoutmaster)[1][3][4]
- **Den Chief** (appointed by Scoutmaster/Cubmaster; serves with Cub pack)[1][2][3]
- **Junior Assistant Scoutmaster** (appointed by Scoutmaster; age 16+)[1][2][3][4]
- **Webmaster** (appointed by SPL/Scoutmaster)[1][2]
- **Outdoor Ethics Guide** (appointed by Scoutmaster)[1][3]

**Notes on troop positions:**
- **Bugler/Musician** counts for Star and Life but **not Eagle**[3]
- **Assistant Patrol Leader** and **Patrol Quartermaster** do **not** count[1]
- Troops may set qualifications (e.g., First Class rank, ILST training, 75% attendance), but official credit requires Scoutmaster verification of active service[2][3]

### Non-Troop Positions (Count if Serving a Linked Unit)
These roles outside the troop count with Scoutmaster approval:
- **Positions in a Venturing crew**: President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Den Chief, Historian, Guide, Quartermaster, Chaplain Aide, Outdoor Ethics Guide[8]
- **Webelos Den Chief** (with Cub pack; age 14+)[2]

### Minimum Service Time
| Rank   | Time Required                  | Details |
|--------|--------------------------------|---------|
| **Star** | 4 months                      | Serve actively in one approved position, verified by Scoutmaster[1][2][3] |
| **Life** | 4 months (additional to Star) | Another approved position; bugler allowed[1][3] |
| **Eagle**| 6 months (total, may overlap) | Any approved position except bugler; must show leadership impact[1][3][8] |

**Recent Changes:** No updates to the approved list appear post-2024 in provided sources (e.g., August 2024 VAHC checklists mentioned, but unrelated to positions). Always check the current Scouts BSA Requirements book or Scouting.org for council-specific guidance, as troops interpret consistently with national standards[1][5][8]
