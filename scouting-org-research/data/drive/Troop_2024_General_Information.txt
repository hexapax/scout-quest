5. Merit Badges - Troop leadership will try to inform Scouts of upcoming merit badge opportunities. However, you are also welcome to research opportunities on your own. Scouts pursue merit badges at their own speed as their schedules allow. There are more than 135 merit badges to choose from. A total of 21 merit badges must be earned for the Eagle Scout rank, including these required 14 merit badges: (a) First Aid, (b) Citizenship in the Community, (c) Citizenship in the Nation, (d) Citizenship in Society, (e) Citizenship in the World, (f) Communication, (g) Cooking, (h) Personal Fitness, (i) Emergency Preparedness OR Lifesaving, (j) Environmental Science OR Sustainability, (k) Personal Management, (l) Swimming OR Hiking OR Cycling, (m) Camping, and (n) Family Life.


5. Cost - The total cost for one year of Boy Scouts (through Dec 2023) at Pace Academy is $250, which is comprised of a $75 national registration fee paid to Boy Scouts of America, and an internal activity fee of $175 meant to cover the cost of our specific Troop activities throughout the year (ie. pizza at meetings, food & sites for campouts, etc). Scouts who have transitioned into Troop 2024 from Pace Cub Scout Pack 4 have already paid the $75 for their Boy Scouts of America membership, so their additional cost due is $175. If a boy is wholly new to Cub Scouts/Boy Scouts, they will also incur a $25 one-time registration fee when they join Boy Scouts of America. Boys moving up from Cub Scouts would not incur this $25 fee. Occasionally we will organize special activities to be paid by participating scouts - for example, our Jan 8th Climbing outing at Stone Summit.
Questions? Contact Parkey parkeyjordan@bellsouth.net or 404-376-9124 cell.


6. Uniforms - You will need to purchase an official BSA uniform, which is referred to as the “Class A” uniform. Class A uniforms will be worn for any Court of Honor scheduled by our Troop, and will occasionally be required to attend Scouting events such as merit badge clinics and ceremonies during Summer Camp. Class A uniforms will not be required at our regular bi-weekly Troop 2024 meetings (unless a Court of Honor has been scheduled).
If you visit the Atlanta Area Boy Scout shop located at 1800 Circle 75 Pkwy, Atlanta, GA 30339, they can help ensure you purchase all the uniform pieces you need.
In addition to the Class A uniform, your son will be provided a Troop 2024 “Class B” shirt, which can be worn at regular Troop meetings.
