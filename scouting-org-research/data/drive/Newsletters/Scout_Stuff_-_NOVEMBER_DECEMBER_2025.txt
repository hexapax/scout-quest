PAYMENT DUE:
“Troop Dues” for calendar year 2026 are now due.  The dues for 2026 will remain at $250.  Please send payment to our troop Treasurer: Eric Buffenbarger.

If you'd like to mail a check, please make it out to Pace Academy and mail to Eric Buffenbarger 865 Moores Mill Road NW, Atlanta, GA 30327-1526

If you prefer, you can also Venmo Eric: @Eric-Buffenbarger. Venmo worked well for most last year as it minimizes risk of checks getting lost in the mail and is easy to track. 

Please note that our Troop 2024 activity dues are different from annual registration fees that are paid directly to Scouting America. Our Troop dues are used to pay for campsite fees, groceries & firewood for campouts, first aid supplies, registration of adults chaperoning Scout events such as Woodruff Scout camp, supplies used for Troop Meetings, pizza served at Troop Meetings, Troopmaster & Teamsnap software usage annual fees, etc. If you have any questions about what our dues cover, feel free to reach out to our troop Treasurer via ericbuffenbarger@reaventures.com

ADULT VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITY:


There are plenty of ways to contribute to the troop even if you don’t want to camp. For example, we strive to have each registered adult in our troop become a Merit Badge Counselor for one of the 14 Eagle-required merit badges. If you would be willing to do this, please let us know. Typically the materials are already prepared for you. The time commitment would be approximately 6 hours total every year (initial, one-time only preptime 3 hours; 2 hours duration of annual MB class;1 hour administrative followup during the year.) Service Hours Reminder Any service hours for rank advancement must be approved in advance by our Advancement Chair or Scoutmaster, unless it is already a Scout troop, district, or council event (ie. Conservation Weekend, Troop 370 MB Clinic, Cub-a-Ree, etc), OR it is an official ICGL-sponsored service event. 
Reimbursement Requests Please submit any reimbursement requests to Eric Buffenbarger at ericbuffenbarger@reaventures.com.  When submitting a reimbursement request, please attach the invoice or receipt reflecting the amount of the reimbursement.  The amount of reimbursement should match the invoice or receipt, so please do not intermingle scouting expenses with personal expenses (e.g., grocery store receipt that includes personal shopping).  Also each item and cost must be legible - might be better to scan/email receipts rather than take pictures of receipts.
